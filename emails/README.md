## Tips for sending

- **Personalize the first name** — these are dads from your community, not a bulk list
- **Keep send times consistent** — Sunday evenings or Monday mornings work well for dad-focused content
- **Forward-ability** — every email should be worth forwarding to one other dad who needs it
- **The book is the credibility anchor** — keep referencing it. If they haven't read it, they'll feel the gap
- **Reply-to engagement** — end most emails with a question that invites a real conversational reply, not just "ok"
